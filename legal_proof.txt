Is it legal to scrape?
Yes, scraping the public pages of uzairways.com for this academic project appears to be allowed, based
on the following evidence:

robots.txt: The file only disallows access to backend and administrative paths such as /admin/, /user/,
and /node/add/. All pages I plan to scrape — including /en/schedule, /en/our-fleet,
/en/press-center/news, and /en/representative-oﬃces — fall outside these restrictions and hence
permitted for access.

Terms of Use: uzairways.com does not appear to have any Terms of Use or Terms of Service. This means
Uzbekistan Airways has not published any contractual terms that would prohibit scraping.
Copyright notice: The only relevant statement on the site is the footer notice: "At a reprint the active link
to the publication is required." Which means that as long as I link back to the source - I can use this site
data.

Uzbekistan site scraping policies are not very well defined as for example in EU which makes it a good
basis for my solely academic purposes.
